Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs and services

How can I join your programs?

Simply fill out our contact form or visit our office. We'll assess your needs and recommend the most suitable program for you.

Are your services free?

Most of our training programs and consultations are free for local farmers. Some specialized services may have minimal fees.

Can I volunteer with your organization?

Yes! We welcome volunteers with various skills. Contact us to discuss volunteer opportunities and how you can contribute.

How long do your programs take?

Program duration varies - from one-day workshops to multi-month training programs. We'll discuss timelines during your consultation.
