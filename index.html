Our Impact Areas

We focus on three key areas to create lasting positive change in our community

Promoting sustainable land use through the integration of trees and crops to improve soil health, biodiversity, and farmer livelihoods.

Building stronger communities through education, training, and collaborative projects that benefit everyone in Rongai.
