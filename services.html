Our Services & Programs

Comprehensive agroforestry solutions designed to help farmers adopt sustainable practices and improve their livelihoods.

What We Offer

Our integrated approach combines technical expertise, community engagement, and practical training to create lasting impact.

Tree Planting Programs

Access to quality tree seedlings and expert guidance on proper planting techniques for maximum environmental and economic benefits.

  • Free tree seedlings for participating farmers
  • Site-specific species recommendations
  • Planting and maintenance training
  • Long-term monitoring support

Farmer Training

Comprehensive education programs covering sustainable farming practices, agroforestry systems, and modern agricultural techniques.

  • Weekly training workshops
  • Hands-on practical sessions
  • Individual farm consultations
  • Certification programs

Soil Conservation

Soil testing, erosion control, and fertility management services to help farmers maintain healthy, productive land.

  • Free soil testing services
  • Erosion control planning
  • Organic fertilizer production
  • Soil health monitoring

Water Management

Rainwater harvesting, irrigation systems, and water conservation techniques to help farmers cope with climate variability.

  • Rainwater harvesting systems
  • Drip irrigation installation
  • Water conservation training
  • Drought-resistant crop varieties

Market Access

Support in finding markets for agroforestry products and assistance with value addition to increase farmer income.

  • Market linkage programs
  • Value addition training
  • Cooperative formation support
  • Quality certification assistance

Community Development

Programs that strengthen community bonds and promote collective action for sustainable development.

  • Women's empowerment programs
  • Youth engagement initiatives
  • Community resource centers
  • Knowledge sharing networks

How We Work

Our step-by-step approach ensures sustainable and effective implementation

1

Assessment

We begin by visiting your farm to assess soil conditions, existing vegetation, and your specific needs and goals.

2

Planning

Based on our assessment, we develop a customized agroforestry plan that integrates trees with your existing farming system.

3

Implementation

We provide the necessary materials and work alongside you to implement the plan, ensuring proper techniques are used.

4

Training

We provide comprehensive training on maintenance, harvesting, and sustainable management of your agroforestry system.

5

Monitoring

We continue to support you with regular follow-up visits and ongoing technical assistance to ensure long-term success.
