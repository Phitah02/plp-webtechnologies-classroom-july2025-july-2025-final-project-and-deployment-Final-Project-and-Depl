About Our Project

Discover our story, mission, and the passionate team working to create a sustainable future for the Rongai community.

Our Mission

The Rongai Agroforestry Community Project was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to transform the agricultural landscape of Rongai through sustainable agroforestry practices that benefit both the environment and the community.

We believe that by integrating trees with crops and livestock, we can create farming systems that are more productive, environmentally sustainable, and resilient to climate change. Our approach combines traditional knowledge with modern agricultural techniques to help local farmers improve their livelihoods while protecting the natural resources they depend on.

Our History

From humble beginnings to community-wide impact

2021

The Beginning

A group of local farmers and environmental enthusiasts came together to address the challenges of soil degradation and declining crop yields in Rongai.

2022

First Pilot Project

Launched our first agroforestry demonstration plot, training 15 farmers in sustainable land management techniques.

2023

Community Expansion

Expanded to work with 50+ farmers across Rongai, establishing tree nurseries and implementing water conservation systems.

2024

Growing Impact

Today, we continue to grow our impact, with plans to reach 200+ farmers and restore 25 hectares of degraded land.

Our Values
